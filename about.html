Welcome to Agrishield Industries Pvt Ltd

AGRISHIELD® Brand of Agrishield Industries Pvt Ltd located in the Pune District of Maharashtra State, in the Kuruli Industrial Area, Tal- Khed near Chakan. It is well connected by road, rail & air.

Our product range includes Organic Biostimulants, Organic Bio Fertilizers, Adjuvants, Chelated Micronutrients, Water Soluble Fertilizers Formulations (NPK's) Straight Micronutrients & their mixtures, Liquid Fertilizers, Insecticides, Pesticides, Herbicides and various Agrochemicals.

The organisation is committed to delivering world-class agrochemical solutions to farmers across India. Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in Kuruli, Pune ensures the highest quality standards at every stage of production — from raw material sourcing to final packaging.

We believe in building long-term relationships with farmers, dealers, and distributors by offering consistent quality, timely delivery, and expert technical support.

Our Certifications

Organisation is an ISO 9001: 2018 certified, Organic Biofertilizers, Organic Bio stimulants DRC Certified, Micronutrients & Water Soluble Fertilizers Manufacturing license, CIBRC & Maharashtra state manufacturing license of Insecticides etc.
